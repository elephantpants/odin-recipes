Lasagne

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces pacentta minced
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 table spoon olive oil
  • 7 ounces ground beef
  • 6 ounces ground pork
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1 1/2 cups plain tomoato sauce
  • 2 cups broth
  1. Step 1 Saute pancetta in a large stockpot over medium-high heat until fat has rendered, 1 to 2 minutes. Add carrot, celery, onion, and oil. Cook and stir until vegetables have softened and onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add beef and pork. Cook, stirring and mashing meat into small crumbles, until browned, 5 to 7 minutes more.
  2. Step 2 Finish the Lasagne
  3. Step 3 Eat the lasagne
